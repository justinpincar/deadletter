To receive pgp-encrypted messages from users, you can create an account on this page. This site stores only the encrypted message - the plaintext note is never sent to our servers. To decrypt, you will need to have your pgp private key available locally. You can optionally store it client-side on your browser using HTML5 local storage.
  • Alias: Publicly viewable name. Used in your personal URL.
  • Password: Allows access to your account. Does not decrypt your messages.
  • Public Key: PGP public key. Used to encrypt messages sent to you.
