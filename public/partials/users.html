Dead Drop

You can create a dead drop bucket with the form below, and receive pgp-encrypted messages from anonymous users. For example, you can view deadletter's dead drop at {{ deadletterDropUrl }}. To decrypt your messages, you will need to have your pgp private key available locally. You can optionally store your key client-side on your browser using HTML5 local storage.

  • Alias: Publicly viewable name. Used in your bucket URL.
  • Password: Allows access to your message list. Does not decrypt your messages.
  • Public Key: PGP public key. Used to encrypt messages which are then stored in the bucket.
